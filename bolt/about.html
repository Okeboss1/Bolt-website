About Bolt Oyo

Bolt Oyo is a leading ride-hailing service dedicated to providing safe, reliable, and affordable transportation solutions across Oyo State. Our mission is to make urban mobility more accessible and convenient for everyone.

Founded in 2023, Bolt Oyo has quickly become a preferred choice for both riders and drivers in the region. We leverage cutting-edge technology to connect passengers with nearby drivers, ensuring quick pickups and smooth rides to destinations throughout Oyo State.

At Bolt Oyo, we prioritize:

  • Safety: All our drivers undergo rigorous background checks and our app includes safety features for both riders and drivers.
  • Reliability: With a large network of drivers, we ensure you can get a ride whenever you need one.
  • Affordability: We offer competitive rates to make transportation accessible to all.
  • Community: We're committed to creating job opportunities and contributing to the local economy of Oyo State.

Choose Bolt Oyo for your next ride and experience the difference!
